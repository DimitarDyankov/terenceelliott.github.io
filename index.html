About us

T.S. Elliott Bookkeeping is run by Terence Elliott MAAT, an AAT Licensed Bookkeeper based in Leighton Buzzard.

A Member of the AAT, Terence has worked in Industry for the past 8 years +, and brings a wealth of experience into practice work.

Outside of accountancy, Terence is both a film and music fanatic, alongside owning three energtic spaniels with his fiance Victoria.

Terence's USP is that he is an accountant with personality! It's hard to find finance people who speak your language and who are genuinely here to help you run your business.

That's where Terence can help by making the numbers work for you and always keeping you books balanced.
